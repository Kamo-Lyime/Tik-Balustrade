Privacy Policy

At Tik Balustrade, based in Pretoria, South Africa, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Details: Name, phone number, and email address.
  • Address: Physical address for site visits and installations.
  • Measurements: Details and dimensions required for accurate quoting.
  • Installation Preferences: Your choices regarding materials and scheduling.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate quotes and respond to your inquiries.
  • To schedule site visits and installations at your convenience.
  • To send updates regarding your project status or changes.
  • To comply with legal obligations under South African law.

How We Protect Your Information

  • Your information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel.
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties except as required by law.
  • We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information.
